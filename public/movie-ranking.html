The best movies that I watched in 2024 - Jishnu

My top three movies

Manjummel Boys

Manjummel boys' adventure takes a dangerous turn when one of them plunges into Guna Caves, igniting a test of unity in a fight for survival.

Malaikottai Vaaliban

Malaikottai Vaaliban, an aging legendary warrior, goes from one adventure to another with his posse. On one such adventure, he encounters Chamathakan, a spiteful man, who swears revenge on him to settle a personal score, changing their lives forever.

Aavesham

Three teens come to Bangalore for their engineering education and get involved in a fight. They find a local gangster to help them.
